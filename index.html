Select Your Difficulty

From Easy to Expert — It’s Your Choice

Jigsaw Puzzles – Daily Puzzle offers 20 different difficulty levels, ranging from just 9 pieces for a quick relaxing break to an impressive 625 pieces for dedicated puzzle enthusiasts.
You can further personalize the challenge by choosing from 7 distinct puzzle piece shapes, adding variety and depth to each session. Want an extra layer of realism? Turn on piece rotation to recreate the authentic tabletop puzzling experience.
Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned solver, there’s always a challenge that fits your mood and skill.

Customize
Your Puzzle Experience

Make the Game Truly Yours

This isn’t just a puzzle game — it’s a fully customizable puzzling environment.
Pick from 25 different backgrounds - find one that doesn't strain your eyes or just looks nice. Decide whether to use the tray or keep all pieces visible. Scatter pieces in an organized way or let them spread randomly for a more natural challenge.

Helpful features include:

  • Shadow Preview option for a transparent guide
  • Show all pieces or edge pieces only
  • Adjustable preview window you can resize and move
  • Large zoomable canvas for detailed precision
  • Screen lock option to prevent accidental movement
  • Music and sound controls

Okay, we're total perfectionists and probably spent way too much time obsessing over tiny details, but hey - it works really well now.

Free Daily Puzzle

A New Puzzle Every Day

Every day brings a fresh challenge under Today’s Free Puzzle in your gallery.
Return daily to enjoy a brand-new image — completely free. It’s a simple and rewarding way to build a relaxing routine and keep your puzzling streak alive.

Puzzles in Progress

Continue Anytime

All unfinished puzzles are automatically saved under In Progress.
Pause your session and return later without losing progress. You can manage your saved puzzles easily — continue where you left off or delete the ones you no longer wish to complete.
No pressure - work on it whenever you feel like it.

Enjoy extended gameplay without worrying about your device’s battery. So you can puzzle for hours without your phone dying.
